App Description

VR Lab Safety is an innovative application designed to teach users about safety protocols in a virtual reality environment. By immersing users in realistic lab scenarios, the app provides an engaging and interactive way to learn important safety measures. Users can connect their VR headsets to their PCs to access the app, and for Oculus Quest headsets, the PC Link feature is required. The app automatically boots up when the executable file is run, offering a seamless experience. If users encounter any issues with their view or position, they can easily reset their view using the Oculus button on their right controller. VR Lab Safety combines fun and education, making it an ideal tool for learning safety protocols in a virtual setting.
